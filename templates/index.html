Welcome to G|DayX 2013

GDayX is a country level community event organized by Google Developer Group, Google Business Group and Google Student Ambassadors in Vietnam inspired by Google G|Days.
  • For Students
  • For Developers
  • For Businesses

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API,YouTube API and Google Calendar API.

Google Developer Group Vietnam is run by passionate individuals in the local developer community where they create an environment to learn about Google Technologies and Tools for developers. This also allows one to see what local companies and developers are doing with these technologies.

The official page is on Google+ GDG Vietnam has a Facebook page and are also on Twitter.

GDG Manager: George Nguyen. Organizers: George Nguyen

GBG Ho Chi Minh City is a community of professionals based in Vietnam aiming to promote the Internet and online services among entrepreneurs, IT professionals, students and other enthusiasts. GBG Ho Chi Minh City, with the primary focus on Google products and services, hopes to encourage the sharing of knowledge and expertise within the community as well as with other GBGs and Google Inc. to gain a clear competitive advantage for its members.

The official page is on Google+ GBG Ho Chi Minh City has a Facebook group, LinkedIn group and are also on Twitter.

GBG Ho Chi Minh City Manager : Dung Go. Organizers: Dung Go
